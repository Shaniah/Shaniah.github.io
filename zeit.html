ABOUT

ROLES: UI/UX DESIGNER, ILLUSTRATOR, BRAND IDENTITY

Futuristic time travel site that boasts luxurious and utility packages for adventurous travelers.

INTRODUCTION

Zeit is looking to start up a new business for booking time travel resort trips to the past.*

*This was a prompt for UX Academy intended to simulate designing a product that does not yet exist - similar to working for a start-up.
THE PROBLEM

Time travel tourism is a fresh market with a lot of unknowns. Many might not trust it or feel safe booking tickets.

CONSTRAINTS
  • Tourists can only travel to specific locations in the past, where Zeit has their resorts in place.
  • Must be able to pull in a wide audience, not just exclusive to wealthy individuals.
  • Research will be prone to bias due to scope of this project.
RESEARCH
INTERVIEWS

The interview consisted of 4 participants, ages 22-29, men and women, across 3 different countries. There were 7 questions asked with follow up questions to collect qualitative data.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Competitive analysis was conducted to find out what features and design choices were made on other travel related sites to ease the process on Zeit’s site.

CARD SORT

The card sort unearthed some very interesting categories to filter with on the site: Political, Art, Science, Environmental, Educational, Cultural, Geography, Religious, and Defiance. (Environmental and Defiance were not consistent, however).

KEY TAKEAWAYS
  • There needs to be different package options for different incomes - add ons for luxury guests.
  • It must feel safe to book with Zeit.
  • The process must be streamlined to lessen confusion for new travelers.
AUDIENCE

People that would be interested in time travel - which are one of two types. Either wealthy and looking for a luxurious experience, or less well off but willing to save up for the adventurous intrigue.

WIREFRAMES

Initial layout and design with a focus on conceptualizing the main pages rather than smaller interactions (based on sketches, sitemap, and user flow).

BRANDING

The branding started by analyzing many fonts that had futuristic or digital elements to them. Through iteration, this sleek and simple design was created.

DESIGN

Orange provides an adventurous but friendly message, while the black and white brings a sleek and futuristic vibe.

THE PRODUCT

From usability testing, a few edits were made to the original design to provide the main pages of a time travel site.

SUMMARY
  • The final site has a futuristic feel and experience, while offering both luxury and utility lodging options for all incomes.
  • Safety and FAQ is available for anyone who is interested, but needs a little more information to take the next step.
  • The branding reflects the adventurous nature of time travel and is cohesive throughout the site.
  • Checking out is extremely simple and minimalist to ensure travelers can book their package seamlessly.
