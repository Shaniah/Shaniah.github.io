ABOUT

ROLES: UI/UX DESIGNER, ILLUSTRATOR, BRAND IDENTITY

Small business creating eco-friendly handmade journals and digital planners.

INTRODUCTION

Dimsy is a small business looking to begin their online shop selling eco-friendly handmade journals and digital products. Their focus is on a whimsical and eclectic aesthetic.

THE PROBLEM

The client does not have any branding for her desired company, and has not created an Esty yet. She needs help starting up and creating a brand she can be proud of and excited about.

CONSTRAINTS
  • Working with a real client means providing full communication and understanding their vision.
  • Must feel like a trustworthy eCommerce site, so users do not feel uneasy purchasing from them.
  • Small businesses have a lot of competition to outmatch online.
RESEARCH
SURVEY

The survey consisted of 56 participants, ages 18-45, of all genders. There were __ questions asked in this survey to obtain quantitative data. (These 3 questions represent only a portion of the survey).

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Competitive analysis was conducted to find out direct and indirect competitors to find ways to stand out. Having a unique storefront allows the client’s site to stand out among other eCommerce sites with independent sellers.

INTERVIEWS

The interview consisted of 4 participants, ages 22-25, men and women, across 3 different countries. There were 7 questions asked with follow up questions to collect qualitative data.

KEY TAKEAWAYS
  • Most participants like to purchase directly from a small business, rather than a big company like Etsy.
  • It is preferred to have coupons available, but not in invasive ways (i.e. popups).
  • Minimalist and easy to use websites that feel trustworthy are important.
IDEATION

People who want to purchase a unique product or gift and would rather support small businesses.

Initial layout and design of the site with a focus on the base pages necessary for an eCommerce site (based on sketches, sitemap, and user flow).

BRANDING

The client wanted something minimalistic while also whimsical/eclectic, which proved difficult - but we eventually decided upon something she can also use as a stamp in her products.

DESIGN

An earthy/golden color pallete based on the client’s preferences and to evoke an eco-friendly, but vintage feeling.
